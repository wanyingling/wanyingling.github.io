For the most part, my work involves analyzing census data and social survey data, and I have contributed to the design and data collection of several survey projects. Additionally, I have collected some social media text and image data, though I seldom use it. Beyond traditional census and survey data, I am also interested in exploring new forms of data, such as images and sounds, in population studies. However, my experience in these areas is still limited.

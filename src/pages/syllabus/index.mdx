## Sociology of Age

### Week 1: Introduction: Why Does Age Matter in Society?

### Week 2: Chronological Age: A Demographic Perspective

### Week 3: Biological Age: Insights from Public Health Disciplines

### Week 4: Life Stages and Age: Exploring Growth and Role Transitions

### Week 5: Generational Differences: Understanding Gen X, Y, Z and Beyond

### Week 6: Age and Performance: "Acting Your Age"

### Week 7: Age and Identity: Perception and Reality of Being Young or Old

### Week 8: Age and Institutions: Exploring Age Structure and Policies

### Week 9: Age and the Marketplace: Age Discrimination and Inequality

### Week 10: Age in Cultural Context: Intersections with Race and Ethnicity

### Week 11: Cultural Aspects of Age: The Impact of Surnames and First Names

### Week 12: Conclusion: The Social Construction of Age

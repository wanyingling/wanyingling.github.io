## Sociology of Age

The *Sociology of Age* course offers an in-depth exploration of aging from a variety of social perspectives. This approach engages with age as a complex construct, showcasing its implications on a personal level and its widespread societal impacts. The journey through the course encompasses diverse areas including an analysis of demographic trends, insights from public health on aging, life stage transitions, and the unique experiences of different generations.

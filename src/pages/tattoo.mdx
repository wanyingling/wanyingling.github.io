Although I primarily engage in quantitative research, I also have a qualitative research project on tattooing underway. This long-term side project focuses on studying tattoo artists and tattooists in mainland China and Hong Kong SAR. I aim to use this project to: 1) supplement the micro-perspective in my research agenda; 2) explore research methodologies, particularly through the use of film and video anthropology, including pictures and video recordings; 3)... This is an interesting and yet little-studied topic in China, isn't it?
